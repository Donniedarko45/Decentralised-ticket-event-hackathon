Frequently Asked Questions

How does blockchain ticketing prevent fraud?

+

Each ticket is a unique NFT with verifiable ownership history recorded immutably on the blockchain, making counterfeiting impossible.

What wallets can I use?

+

We support MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet, and other popular Web3 wallets for seamless integration.

How do secondary royalties work?

+

Smart contracts automatically distribute a percentage of resale value to original creators/organizers, creating ongoing revenue streams.

Is it mobile-friendly?

+

Yes! Our platform works seamlessly on mobile devices with QR/NFC scanning for easy event entry.

What about gas fees?

+

We use Base blockchain for ultra-low transaction costs (under $0.01) compared to expensive Ethereum mainnet fees.
